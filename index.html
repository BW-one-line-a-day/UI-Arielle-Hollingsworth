What is One Line A Day?

This is the app version of the 10 year one line a day journal. Your daily entries for each day over 10 years are recorded, and you are reminded each day to record a short and sweet fact from your day- a funny quote, crazy milestone, or what you ate for dinner.

One Line A Day is your everyday journal.
